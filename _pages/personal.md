## Woodworking Projects

### Custom Walnut Desk Build

Built a custom solid walnut desk from scratch, featuring hand-cut joinery and a natural oil finish.

**Materials Used:**
- Solid walnut lumber
- Danish oil finish
- Brass hardware

**Process:**
1. Milled lumber and cut to dimensions
2. Created mortise and tenon joints
3. Assembly and gluing
4. Sanding (up to 320 grit)
5. Applied multiple coats of Danish oil
