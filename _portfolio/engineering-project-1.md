## Overview
This project focused on developing and implementing consensus control algorithms for multi-robot systems under communication delays.

## Key Technologies
- Python, C++, MATLAB
- ROS (Robot Operating System)
- Control Theory & Optimization
- Hardware: Differential-wheeled robots

## Achievements
- Designed predictor-based controllers for fast consensus
- Validated algorithms through hardware experiments
- Published results in IEEE conferences and journals
